Hey, I'm Nazmul

Unlike the odds, I am quite design-minded and detail oriented software engineer. I am extremely passionate about combining beautiful code with beautiful design.

Recently, I have graduated with a 1:1 degree in Computer Science, and started my career as a Junior Software Developer for a financial services company @ FEXCO.

Right now, I am developing a cash out solution for the Philippines, called EasyDebit. My career allows me to create software that not only functions efficiently under the hood, but also provides intuitive user experiences.

My goal is to develop my knowledge of Java, Spring Boot and AngularJS.

However, I am also in constant search to broaden my interest in mobile app development and computer security.

I love being challenged. The best way I learn is by doing and "messing things up". Being a painful perfectionist, I take my 9-5 work as an ongoing education, and I'm always looking for opportunities to work with those who are willing to share their knowledge as much as I want to learn. At the end of the day, my primary goal is to create something beautiful and useful, with people that bring that best in me.

When I am not typing with my 'split keyboard', I'm probably cooking, photographing, travelling, listening to self-help podcasts, or expanding my knowledge of Islam.
